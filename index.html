Mike Cornelia

0-to-1 builder. Technology and the people who run it.

Two decades doing two things: building technology that didn’t exist before, and building teams that produce work nobody thought possible.

They’re not separate skills. The same instincts that make me a good systems engineer make me a good leader: pattern recognition, root-cause thinking, the discipline to kill a bad bet early.

I love the technology. I love the people. I’ve never bought into the idea that you can be great at one without the other.
